Lesson 1: Introduction to Programming

The purpose of this lesson is to introduce some basic concepts of computer programming. You will be introduced to the general concepts of computer programming, the names of some programming languages, and the two major categories of programs, procedural and object-oriented. You will also be introduced to the three control structures (Sequence, Selection, and Iteration) and how they are used in all computer programs.

Learning Objectives

  • Objective 1 Describe the evolution of computer programming languages.
  • Objective 2 Differentiate between script kiddies, hackers, and programmers.
  • Objective 3 Differentiate between procedural and object-oriented languages.
  • Objective 4 Differentiate between the three control structures: Sequence, Selection, and Iteration and describe the differences between the three.
  • Objective 5 Identify the five phases of the Software Development Life Cycle and explain why they are important.
